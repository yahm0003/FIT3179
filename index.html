Healthcare Expenditure in Australia

Geographical Distribution

This map highlights the healthcare expenditure across different states in Australia. It provides a visual representation of how healthcare funds are distributed geographically.

The map is color-coded to show varying levels of expenditure, with a detailed breakdown by region.

Expenditure Over Time

The line chart represents the total healthcare expenditure over the years, segmented by different areas of expenditure. This helps to track trends in healthcare funding and monitor changes over time.

Use the filter to focus on specific areas of healthcare expenditure, such as administration or aids and appliances.

Breakdown by Funding Source

The bar chart illustrates the distribution of healthcare funds based on the sources of funding, such as government contributions, private insurance, and other non-government sources.

This breakdown provides insights into how different sectors contribute to overall healthcare spending in Australia.

Funding Allocation

The donut chart shows how healthcare funds are allocated across different areas of expenditure. This visualization helps identify which areas receive the most financial support, such as hospital services, primary care, or public health programs.
